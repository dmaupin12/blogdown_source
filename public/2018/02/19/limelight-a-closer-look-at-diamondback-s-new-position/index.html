Limelight: A Closer Look at Diamondback's New Position

February 19, 2018 in Peer Analysis
Focus
  • Diamondback recently announced a new exploration play targeting the Mississippian oil play in Ector and Crane Counties.
  • Company acquired ~19,000 acres which they plan on delineating beginning this year.
  • The map below identifies offsetting permit/spud activity.
    • Majority of activity is vertical development, with the most active operator, Oxy, North of their acreage.
    • Adventure Operating has completed 2 horizontal wells on their acreage in 2016 and 2017 targeting the San Andreas formation.
      • Edwards 31 8HA was completed with a 4,866’ lateral and frac’d with 655 lbs/ft of proppant.
        • The well IP’d at 88 Bbl/d and 22 Mcf/d and has produced ~57 Mbo in 15 months.
      • Edwards 31 1HA was completed with a 5,390’ lateral and frac’d with 343 lbs/ft of proppant.
        • The well IP’d at 107 Bbl/d and 65 Mcf/d and has produced ~14 Mbo in 5 months.
  • In Crane County, there is a single active horizontal permit.

EIA DUC Report

 Apr 4, 2018
Overview of EIA DUC Inventory Report A drilled but uncompleted well (DUC) is a new well after the end of the drilling process, but its first completion process has not been concluded. EIA uses 20 days to estimate days to TD. This likely overestimates DUC inventory in basins where PAD drilling is predominant. Report tracks monthly changes in DUC inventory beginning in 2014. Permian Monthly Highlights Permian Basin ended February with ~3,044 wells waiting on completion.

Oil and Gas Producer Pricing Index

 Apr 4, 2018
Producer Pricing Index What does it measure? The Producer Price Index (PPI) program measures the average change over time in the selling prices received by domestic producers for their output. The prices included in the PPI are from the first commercial transaction for many products and some services. Why is it useful? Commonly used in escalating purchase and sales contracts. Indicator of overall price movement at the producer level.
