Focus
  • Diamondback recently announced a new exploration play targeting the Mississippian oil play in Ector and Crane Counties.
  • Company acquired ~19,000 acres which they plan on delineating beginning this year.
  • The map below identifies offsetting permit/spud activity.
    • Majority of activity is vertical development, with the most active operator, Oxy, North of their acreage.
    • Adventure Operating has completed 2 horizontal wells on their acreage in 2016 and 2017 targeting the San Andreas formation.
      • Edwards 31 8HA was completed with a 4,866’ lateral and frac’d with 655 lbs/ft of proppant.
        • The well IP’d at 88 Bbl/d and 22 Mcf/d and has produced ~57 Mbo in 15 months.
      • Edwards 31 1HA was completed with a 5,390’ lateral and frac’d with 343 lbs/ft of proppant.
        • The well IP’d at 107 Bbl/d and 65 Mcf/d and has produced ~14 Mbo in 5 months.
  • In Crane County, there is a single active horizontal permit.
