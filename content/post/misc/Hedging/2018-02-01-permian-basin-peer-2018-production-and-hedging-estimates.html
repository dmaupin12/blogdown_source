Methodology and Focus

  • Sourced data from a combination of investor presentations, quarterly reports and FactSet.
  • Summarize company hedging profiles for Permian Basin operators.
  • Identify which companies are at risk from price inflation or deflation.
  • Determine oil production estimates for 2018.

Highlights

  • Of the companies which produce more than 100 MBbls/d, only Pioneer and Concho have hedged more than 50% of 2018 production.
  • Parsley stands out with ~100% of 2018 oil hedged.
  • On the opposite end of the spectrum, EOG and Centennial have no hedges in place and Apache has ~1%.
  • Companies have locked in ~45% of 2018 estimated oil production at an average price of $51.
