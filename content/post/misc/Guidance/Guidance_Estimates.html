Methodology

  • Analyzed thirty companies with significant operations in the Permian Basin.
  • Calculated expected oil growth rates from the market through 2019.
  • Sourced data from FactSet.

Highlights

  • Year-end proved reserves for Concho totaled ~841 MMboe which put the company in the top 10 in total reserves.
    • Proved oil reserves made up ~60% of the total.
    • Undeveloped reserves totaled ~253 MMboe, or 30% of the total.
  • In contrast to the peer group, Pioneer only shows 8% of undeveloped reserves.
    • Likely not a read-through to inventory, however it’s interesting to see the difference in PUD strategy.
  • Before accounting for the RSP acquisition, Concho’s mean estimate for year-end 2019 daily oil rate is 192 Mbo/d, a 47% increase from year-end 2017 rates.
    • Post acquisition and without accounting for operational efficiencies, the street expects the combined entity will produce 270 Mbo/d, or 55% more than year-end 2017 rates.
  • The only company expected to grow oil production faster than Concho by 2019 is Diamondback.
